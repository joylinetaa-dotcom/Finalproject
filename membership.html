Why Join Us?

Becoming a member of Scarlets Hockey Club gives you access to regular training sessions, friendly matches, tournaments, coaching support, and a strong team environment.

  • Weekly hockey training sessions
  • Friendly and competitive matches
  • Beginner-friendly coaching
  • Opportunities to join club teams
  • Supportive sports community
