Our Teams

Scarlets Hockey Club has teams for different age groups and skill levels.

Men’s Team

The men’s team represents Scarlets HC in competitive league matches and friendly tournaments. Players train weekly to improve speed, passing, shooting, and tactical awareness.

  • Training: Tuesday and Thursday
  • Level: Intermediate to advanced
  • Focus: Competitive performance

Women’s Team

The women’s team gives players a platform to grow in confidence, compete actively, and represent the club in local competitions.

  • Training: Monday and Wednesday
  • Level: Beginner to advanced
  • Focus: Skill development and teamwork

Junior Team

The junior team introduces young players to hockey basics in a safe and supportive environment. Training focuses on ball control, movement, discipline, and fun.

  • Training: Saturday morning
  • Level: Beginners
  • Focus: Fundamentals and confidence
